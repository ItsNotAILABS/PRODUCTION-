# Medina Protocol Charter Index v1

**Status:** Active protocol charter  
**Owner:** Alfredo Medina Hernandez / MedinaTech Research / MedinaSITech / ItsNotAILABS  
**Purpose:** Define protocol names, Latin names, placement, authority, and operating charters for the Medina research, execution, routing, proof, publication, licensing, and release stack.

## Foundational Doctrine

```text
AURO speaks.
ORIGO builds.
THESIS proves, protects, captures, hashes, notarizes, and publishes.
TERMINUS executes and organizes.
ORO orients.
ORDO orders.
MEMORIA remembers.
REGISTRUM records.
ARTIFACTA bodies the output.
```

## Protocol Family (v1)

### 1) AURO — Protocolum Vocis Auri
- **Name:** AURO Speaking Intelligence Protocol
- **Role:** Final synthesis and public-safe expression.
- **Canonical line:** `AURO speaks Medina intelligence.`

### 2) ORIGO — Protocolum Originis Operum
- **Name:** ORIGO Build and Implementation Protocol
- **Role:** Build runtime bodies, SDKs, schemas, interfaces, and deployment systems.
- **Canonical line:** `ORIGO builds Medina systems.`

### 3) THESIS — Protocolum Thesis: De Probatione, Tutela, Publicatione et Notariatu
- **Name:** THESIS Research, IP, Proof, Publication, and Notarization Protocol
- **Role:** Claim hardening, evidence binding, hashing, notarization readiness, release gating.
- **Authority states:**
  - `DRAFT`
  - `INTERNAL_RESEARCH`
  - `CLAIM_HARDENED`
  - `HASHED`
  - `NOTARY_READY`
  - `COUNSEL_READY`
  - `PUBLIC_SAFE`
  - `PRIVATE_VAULT`
  - `DEPLOYMENT_READY`
- **Canonical line:** `THESIS proves, protects, captures, hashes, notarizes, and publishes.`

### 4) TERMINUS — Protocolum Terminorum: De Superficie Stationis Nativae
- **Name:** TERMINUS Native Workstation Surface Protocol
- **Role:** Execution session organization, command/event handling, routing to memory/records/artifacts.
- **Canonical line:** `TERMINUS executes and organizes.`

### 5) RATIO ORDINIS — De Substratis Neuralibus et Reticularibus
- **Name:** RATIO ORDINIS Neuro-Network Routing Protocol
- **Subprotocols:** ORO (orientation), ORDO (ordering)
- **Role:** Route selection, risk scoring, route cards, fallback planning, consequence awareness.
- **Canonical line:** `ORO orients. ORDO orders.`

### 6) MEMORIA — Protocolum Memoriae: De Linea et Consequentia
- **Name:** MEMORIA Lineage and Consequence Protocol
- **Role:** Durable, append-only canonical lineage and consequence memory.
- **Canonical line:** `MEMORIA remembers consequence.`

### 7) REGISTRUM — Protocolum Registri: De Signis, Schematibus et Tabulis Canonicis
- **Name:** REGISTRUM Canonical Record Protocol
- **Role:** Schema-defined canonical IDs, ledgers, and audit records.
- **Canonical line:** `REGISTRUM records canonical structure.`

### 8) ARTIFACTA — Protocolum Artifactorum: De Corporibus Durabilibus
- **Name:** ARTIFACTA Durable Artifact Protocol
- **Role:** Durable bodies for papers, packages, bundles, evidence packets, and release artifacts.
- **Canonical line:** `ARTIFACTA gives output a body.`

### 9) SVA — Protocolum Validationis SVA: De Assertionibus et Evidentiis
- **Name:** SVA Claim and Evidence Review Protocol
- **Role:** Claim class validation (C1–C10), evidence gating, public-safe wording controls.
- **Taxonomy note:** C1–C10 are the SVA release-gating claim classes used by THESIS/SVA validation workflows.
- **Canonical line:** `SVA hardens claims before release.`

### 10) AUTE — Protocolum Experimentorum AUTE: De Probationibus et Tentaminibus
- **Name:** AUTE Experiment and Test Design Protocol
- **Role:** Convert unsupported claims into testable experiments and reproducibility plans.
- **Canonical line:** `AUTE tests what THESIS cannot yet prove.`

### 11) License Registry — Registrum Licentiarum Medinae
- **Name:** Medina License Registry Protocol
- **Role:** Rights boundaries for research, production, deep research, evidence graph, and collaborator review.
- **Canonical line:** `Public release does not mean open license.`

### 12) Zenodo Release — Protocolum Editionis Zenodo: De DOI, Praeprintis et Provenientia
- **Name:** Zenodo Research Release Protocol
- **Role:** DOI-backed publication discipline and version lineage.
- **Canonical line:** `Each paper gets its own DOI; each version keeps its lineage.`

### 13) Reference Stream — Flumen Referentiarum
- **Name:** Reference Stream Protocol
- **Role:** Metadata-level citation lineage between related releases.
- **Canonical line:** `Reference stream connects the record to its lineage.`

### 14) Production Repository — Protocolum Repositorii Productionis
- **Name:** ItsNotAILABS Production Repository Protocol
- **Repository:** `ItsNotAILABS/PRODUCTION-` (exact GitHub repository slug)
- **Required directories:**
  - `/licenses`
  - `/papers`
  - `/protocols`
  - `/schemas`
- **Canonical line:** `PRODUCTION- stages implementation without surrendering rights.`

### 15) Medina Journal — Protocolum Diarii Medinae: De Articulis, Probationibus et Editione
- **Name:** Medina Journal and Publication Office Protocol
- **Role:** Claim/evidence/hash/release-gated publication office discipline.
- **Canonical line:** `Medina’s journal must be more disciplined than a blog.`

### 16) Grant Radar — Specula Donorum: De Subventionibus et Aptitudine Medinae
- **Name:** THESIS Grant Radar Protocol
- **Role:** Grant scanning, fit scoring, and evidence-aware submission readiness.
- **Canonical line:** `THESIS must know which Medina systems can truthfully support the application.`

### 17) Evidence Graph — Graphum Evidentiae: De Radice Notaria
- **Name:** Evidence Graph and Notary Root Protocol
- **Role:** Bind diagnostics, traces, route cards, claims, artifacts, and hashes into notarizable graph roots.
- **Canonical line:** `The notary should record the root of the evidence graph, not only the final file bundle.`
